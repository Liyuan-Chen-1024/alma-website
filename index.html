The Product

Meet Alma

A mobile AI companion that lives with you, moves with you, and does things with you.

🏠
Mobile Presence
Moves room-to-room — kitchen, living room, bedside. Always there when you need her. Physical co-presence transforms a device into a true companion.
🧠
Deep AI Memory
Powered by Gemini's 1M-token context window. Alma remembers your stories, your family, your preferences — with no "novelty cliff" over time.
🎨
Shared Activities
Cooking, art, music, games, exercise — Alma does things with you, not just talks at you. Six or more collaborative activities proven to reduce loneliness.
🤖
Two Modes, One Companion
Alma adapts seamlessly between warm companionship and attentive care — no awkward mode switching required.
Companion Mode
Warm expressive face, carries tea and everyday items on the open shelf. Focused on connection and joy.
Care Mode
Health dashboard, medication reminders, carries first aid supplies. Quietly vigilant when it matters most.

Under the Hood

Built on Gemini Robotics

Hybrid compute architecture: on-device for privacy, cloud for deep conversation.

Navigation
Gemini Robotics VLA
Vision-Language-Action model for autonomous room-to-room navigation. Fine-tuned with just 100 demos for >70% task success.
50Hz control · 250ms latency
Context Awareness
Gemini Robotics-ER
Embodied Reasoning for situational awareness. "User in kitchen → suggest cooking." 1M token input with full tool calling support.
1M token context window
Conversation
Gemini 2.5 Flash
Weeks of conversational memory in a single context window. Dynamic content generation with a consistent, warm personality layer.
1M token context · Dynamic generation
Privacy
Gemini On-Device
2.38–6.3B parameter model on embedded AI compute. Face and emotion processing stays entirely local — privacy by architecture, not policy.
Privacy-first · No cloud dependency for sensing
